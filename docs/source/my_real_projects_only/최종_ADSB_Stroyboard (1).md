## Summary

Storyboard package for the Andersson Bell GenAI short-form project.

## Project Context

- Industry-academic collaboration focused on producing a brand-fit short-form video for Andersson Bell.
- The project moved from open-ended experimentation toward a controlled storyboard and cut-sequence system.
- Core concept referenced elsewhere in the repo: "Mutated Mundane."

## Direction Captured By The Storyboard

- Started from brand analysis and motif selection rather than making disconnected AI visuals.
- Dropped the earlier collage-heavy direction after it weakened tone, focus, and brand fit.
- Rebuilt the sequence around a restrained fixed-angle structure.
- Used stakeholder feedback as production rules for the next revision cycle.
- Connected image generation and video generation into a repeatable workflow.
